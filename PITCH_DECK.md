## Slide 4: Why It Works

We only allow **5 agents per zip code**. Once a zip is full, new agents go on a waitlist.

- Agents stay because leaving means losing their spot
- Scarcity creates urgency — "my zip is almost full"
- Waitlists build demand before we spend a dollar on ads
- Agents tell other agents, but only in different zip codes

**Built-in retention. Built-in referrals. Built-in pricing power.**
