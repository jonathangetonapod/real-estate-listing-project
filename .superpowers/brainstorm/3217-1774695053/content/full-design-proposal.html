Aesthetic Direction: Polished SaaS + Real Estate Edge

Visual thesis: A dark, high-contrast hero that feels like a premium developer tool (Linear, Vercel), transitioning to clean light sections that feel approachable and warm for non-technical agents. Orange accent cuts through dark UI like a lit window at night. The product feels powerful but not intimidating.

Typography Stack

Display — Cabinet Grotesk (Bold/ExtraBold)
Your Next Listing Is Already Waiting.
Why: Geometric, confident, slightly rounded terminals give warmth without losing authority. Reads as modern SaaS, not generic.
Body — Outfit
ListingPitch finds motivated sellers in your farm area, writes hyper-personalized emails in your voice, and delivers your lead list within 12 hours. You focus on closing deals.
Why: Clean geometric sans with slightly rounded forms. Pairs beautifully with Cabinet Grotesk. Excellent legibility at small sizes.
Data / Metrics — JetBrains Mono
12,847 pitches sent · $485K avg price · 94% match score
Why: Tabular numbers align perfectly. Monospace in data contexts signals precision and technical capability.
