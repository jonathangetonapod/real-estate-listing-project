Design Principles

🏡
Real Estate Warmth
Cream backgrounds, terracotta accents, soft shadows. Premium but approachable.
✨
Generous Whitespace
Sections breathe. 80-120px vertical padding. Nothing feels cramped.
📊
Real-Looking Data
Product mockups use realistic property data, not lorem ipsum. Fake but believable.
🎬
Subtle Motion
Fade-in-up on scroll, counter animations, smooth hover states. No bouncing or flashy transitions.
